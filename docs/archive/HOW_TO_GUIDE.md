This is for the definitions of the indicator fields, linked to scenarios.

Key Metric Definitions:
Strength (1-9): 1 is a "background noise" indicator; 9 is a "system-changing" event.

Time Weight: The typical frequency of reporting or observation required to validate the indicator.

Decay: Linear (relevance fades slowly), Step (relevance drops sharply if a specific event doesn't occur), or None (permanent structural change).
